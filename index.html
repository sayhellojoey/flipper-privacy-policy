Privacy Policy for Flipper for YouTube

Effective Date: March 20, 2026

Flipper for YouTube is architected as a standalone utility. We do not maintain external servers, we do not track your activity, and we have no access to your identity or browsing history.

1. Information We Collect

We strive to collect as little data as possible.

  • Usage Data: We do not collect or store any information about your browsing history or YouTube preferences.
  • Local Storage: Your settings and trial status stay entirely on your local machine and never leave your browser.

2. Permissions

The extension uses storage and host permissions to manage the layout flip and securely verify your license status.
